Nutritionix-tend

Nutritionix-tend makes it easy to know what you're eating, even without a label.

By taking advantage of the powerful Nutritionix API, Nutritionix-tend can fetch nutritional information from nearly a million branded retail foods and restaurant menu offerings. Even without a label, your dietary information is just a couple seconds away.

And if what you're eating isn't already indexed, Nutritionix-tend's integrated recipe builder and knowledge of over 25,000 generic ingredients can help you keep a tally on the nutrition of any novel meal, ingredient by ingredient.
